About

We are a collective of Somali digital nomads. Our community connects, learns and shares the knowledge and skills needed to position Somalis in the new era of Digital Africa and The Fourth Industrial Revolution.

Our mission is to help strengthen youth employability prospects in the modern labour market. Whether you want to launch your own start-up or find the right job, our platform can help you reach your desired goals through practical and relevant methods.

Services

Learn

Life is a continuous learning curve. This is especially true in the technology sector. It’s vitally important to remain current, informed and up-to-date on all the latest technologies. We’re here to help guide you through your ‘Tech school of life’ – where continuous upskilling is a way of life.

Community

Mixed discipline teams = high performing teams. We all have our specialist fields, but when we come together and collaborate, that’s when the magic really happens! Join our social media communities and build innovative solutions together.

Consultancy

Our vastly experienced team consist of experts from an array of business disciplines. We’re here to help you grow as an individual, team or business.
